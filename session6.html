equality and equaty

equality=sameness

Equality means providing the same opportunities and resources to all individuals, regardless of their background or circumstances.
It aims to treat everyone equally and promote justice.
However, it may not address the unique needs and challenges that some individuals face.

equaty=fairness

Equity means ensuring that everyone gets what they need to have an equal chance at success.
It takes into account the diverse backgrounds and challenges individuals may have.
By providing additional support and resources to those who require them,
equity promotes fairness and equal outcomes.
